Singapore

Singapore is a small island city-state located in Southeast Asia. It is known for its multicultural society, modern architecture, and efficient public transportation system. The country has a tropical climate and is famous for its cleanliness and safety. Singapore is also a global financial hub and attracts many expatriates from around the world. The official languages are English, Malay, Mandarin, and Tamil. I have loved in Singapore since 2017, completing 8 years this year.
